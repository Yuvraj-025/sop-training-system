1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the standardized process for onboarding new employees at the company. The purpose is to ensure that every new hire receives a consistent, comprehensive, and welcoming introduction to the organization, its culture, tools, and role-specific responsibilities.

A well-executed onboarding process reduces time-to-productivity, improves employee retention, and ensures compliance with company policies and legal requirements.

2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All full-time and part-time employees joining the organization
- Remote and on-site employees
- HR Business Partners, Department Managers, IT Support, and Payroll teams involved in the onboarding process

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3. RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 Human Resources (HR)
- Coordinate and oversee the entire onboarding process
- Prepare and send offer letters, contracts, and pre-joining documentation
- Schedule orientation sessions
- Maintain onboarding records for audit and compliance

3.2 IT Department
- Set up hardware (laptop, phone, access cards)
- Create user accounts, email IDs, and system access credentials
- Install required software and VPN access
- Provide IT orientation and helpdesk introduction

3.3 Hiring Manager / Department Head
- Prepare a 30-60-90 day onboarding plan for the new employee
- Assign a buddy or mentor from the team
- Schedule 1:1 meetings for the first two weeks
- Conduct role-specific training and project briefings

3.4 Payroll / Finance
- Set up employee records in the payroll system
- Collect and verify tax and banking information
- Ensure first payroll cycle is accurate

4. PRE-JOINING PROCESS (Before Day 1)

Step 1: Send Offer Letter and Documentation (T-14 days)
- HR sends the signed offer letter via email
- Attach: employment contract, NDA, background check consent form
- Request: PAN card, Aadhaar (or equivalent), educational certificates, previous employment records

Step 2: Background Verification (T-7 days)
- Initiate background verification with the third-party vendor (BGV team)
- Verify: Identity, Address, Education, Previous Employment
- Flag any discrepancies to the hiring manager immediately
- Compliance Note: All offers are conditional until BGV clearance

Step 3: IT Setup Request (T-5 days)
- HR raises IT ticket using ServiceNow with new hire details
- Specify: laptop model preference, software licenses needed, system access level
- IT confirms setup availability 2 business days before joining

Step 4: Welcome Email (T-2 days)
- HR sends personalized welcome email with:
  - Office address / remote work instructions
  - Day 1 schedule and reporting time
  - Dress code and parking/access instructions
  - Emergency contact number for Day 1 queries
  - Link to company handbook and culture deck

5. DAY 1 ONBOARDING

Step 5: Welcome and Reception (9:00 AM)
- HR Coordinator receives the new hire at reception
- Provide access card, company ID badge, and welcome kit
- Escort to assigned workstation or meeting room

Step 6: HR Orientation (9:30 AM – 11:00 AM)
- Company overview: Mission, vision, values, org structure
- HR policies: Leave policy, code of conduct, POSH policy, benefits overview
- Employee handbook walkthrough
- Sign off on policy acknowledgment forms

Step 7: IT Onboarding (11:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
- Handover laptop and peripherals
- Set up company email, Slack/Teams, and JIRA/project tools
- Install VPN and walk through security protocols
- Demonstrate password management and 2FA setup

Step 8: Payroll Documentation (12:00 PM – 12:30 PM)
- Collect signed bank details and tax declaration forms
- Add employee to HRMS (e.g., Zoho People / BambooHR)
- Set up payroll entry for the current or next cycle

Step 9: Department Introduction (2:00 PM – 4:00 PM)
- Hiring manager introduces the new hire to the team
- Team lunch or coffee catch-up (virtual or in-person)
- Buddy/mentor introduction and contact sharing
- Overview of team goals, current projects, and team norms

Step 10: Role Briefing (4:00 PM – 5:00 PM)
- Hiring manager shares 30-60-90 day plan
- Role-specific tools and systems walkthrough
- First week agenda shared via calendar invites

6. WEEK 1 ACTIVITIES

- Daily 15-minute check-in with buddy/mentor
- Complete all mandatory training modules on the LMS by Day 5:
  - Information Security Awareness
  - POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment)
  - Data Privacy and GDPR Basics
  - Company Values and Culture Course
- Attend all relevant team standups and meetings (shadowing mode)
- Complete HR forms and submit any remaining documentation
- Set up first 1:1 with direct manager by Day 3

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

7. 30-60-90 DAY MILESTONES

Day 30:
- Complete all mandatory trainings
- Understand team structure and immediate project scope
- Have met all key stakeholders in department
- Manager conducts 30-day check-in meeting

Day 60:
- Begin contributing independently to at least one project
- Deep-dive into 2-3 core tools/processes of the role
- Share feedback on onboarding experience via pulse survey
- Manager review: Is the employee on track? Are there gap areas?

Day 90:
- Fully integrated into team workflows
- Goals for the next quarter set in performance management system
- Formal feedback session with manager
- HR conducts probation review meeting

8. COMPLIANCE AND REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

8.1 Data Privacy
- All personal data collected during onboarding must be stored securely in the HRMS
- Access is restricted to HR, IT, and Payroll only
- Data retention policy: 7 years post-employment for statutory compliance

8.2 Mandatory Trainings
- POSH training must be completed within 7 days of joining (as per The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013)
- Information Security training must be completed within 5 days (as per company ISO 27001 policy)
- Non-completion must be escalated to the HR Head

8.3 Background Verification
- All onboarding offers are conditional on BGV clearance
- Adverse BGV results must be reviewed by Legal and HR before any action

9. ESCALATION MATRIX

| Issue                        | Escalate To             |
|------------------------------|-------------------------|
| BGV discrepancy              | Legal + HR Head         |
| IT setup not ready           | IT Manager              |
| Payroll delay                | Finance Head            |
| Employee concern (Day 1-7)   | HR Business Partner     |
| Policy violation             | HR Head + Legal         |
